Our Mission

SchedUTrack is dedicated to enhancing the academic journey of university students by providing an integrated, user-friendly platform that centralizes academic management. Our mission is to empower students to seamlessly organize their schedules, track attendance, manage tasks, and monitor grades, thereby fostering a productive and successful academic experience.

About Us

Founded at Habib University, SchedUTrack arose from the pressing need to simplify the complexities university students face in managing their academic lives. We understand that juggling multiple courses, deadlines, and attendance can be overwhelming. SchedUTrack consolidates essential academic functions into one accessible platform, enabling students to stay on top of their responsibilities and avoid academic penalties.


Innovation: We strive to continually improve and innovate, ensuring our platform meets the evolving needs of modern students.


Reliability: SchedUTrack is built to be a dependable resource for students, providing accurate and timely information to support their academic endeavors.


User-Centric Design: We prioritize intuitive design and functionality, ensuring that our platform is easy to use for all students, enhancing their organizational skills and academic productivity.
